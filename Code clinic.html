Problems in Healthcare System

  • Long Waiting & Overcrowding – Delays even with prior appointments.
  • Accessibility Issues – Difficult travel for rural & elderly patients, costly transport.
  • High Costs – Consultation, medicines, and hidden charges add up.
  • Short Consultation Time – Doctors rush, patients can’t explain fully.
  • Communication Barriers – Medical jargon & language differences.
  • Privacy Concerns – No private space, hesitation in sharing sensitive issues.
  • Follow-up Difficulties – Revisits needed even for small clarifications.
  • Emotional Barriers – Fear, anxiety, embarrassment reduce honest reporting.
  • Infection Risk – Overcrowding increases chances of catching diseases.
  • Poor Record Management – Lost papers, no centralized medical history.
